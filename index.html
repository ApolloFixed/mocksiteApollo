🚲Urban Tribes Vienna🚲

Community point and self repair bike shop in Vienna

Urban Tribes Vienna was founded in 2017 when a group of messengers decided to creat a community point for all the bike enthousiasts and bike messengesrs to meet.

Events

Urban Tribe has organized a number of events for the bike scene in Vienna. From events especially for bike messengers, to free bike services and also open parties for everyone looking for a good time:

  • Winter Warriors
  • Spring Assembly
  • Gears of the Tribe
  • Halloween Alleycat 2019 - The Summoning of Baphomet
  • Joint bike repair station with SPÖ
  • Cafe Cubano
